## Executive Summary

Guardian Flow is positioned as the premier compliance-ready, AI-powered field service management platform for enterprise organizations in regulated industries. Our business strategy focuses on capturing market share in the $8.5B FSM market by differentiating on compliance automation, fraud prevention, and developer-friendly APIs.

## Risk Assessment

### Business Risks

**1. Market Risk: Economic Downturn**
- **Probability:** Medium (30%)
- **Impact:** High (revenue growth slows)
- **Mitigation:** 
  - Focus on ROI-positive use cases
  - Flexible contract terms (monthly billing option)
  - Diversify across industries

**2. Competitive Risk: Incumbent Response**
- **Probability:** High (70%)
- **Impact:** Medium (pricing pressure)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Maintain innovation velocity
  - Build switching costs (integrations, data lock-in)
  - Patent AI/ML algorithms

**3. Execution Risk: Scaling Challenges**
- **Probability:** Medium (40%)
- **Impact:** High (quality degrades, churn increases)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Invest in platform reliability
  - Hire experienced leadership
  - Implement strong QA processes

### Financial Risks

**1. Cash Flow Risk:**
- Runway: 18 months at current burn rate
- Mitigation: Raise Series A ($20M) Q2 2026

**2. Customer Concentration Risk:**
- Top 10 customers = 40% of revenue
- Mitigation: Diversify customer base, reduce reliance
